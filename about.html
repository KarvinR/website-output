About Us

Mapúa's Bachelor of Science in Computer engineering blends electrical engineering and computer science to prepare you for cutting-edge technological advancements.

The baccalaureate Curriculum Guidelines for Undergraduate Degree Programs insights into the nature of this field:

  • Computer Engineering focuses on the design, construction, implementation, and maintenance of both hardware and software in modern computing system.
  • it combines elements of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering but has evolved into a distinct discipline over the past three decades.
  • Grounded in computing, mathematics, science, and engineering principles, it applies these theories to solve technical problems through hardware, sofrware, networks, and process design.

The Computer Engineering program offers the following academic tracks:

  • Bachelor of Engineering Technology in Computer Engineering Technology
  • Bachelor of Engineering Technology-Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (Fully Online Program)
  • Bachelor of Science-Master of Science in Computer Engineering
  • Master of Science in Computer Engineering
  • Master of Science in Computer Engineering by Research
  • Master of Science in Computer Engineering (Fully Online Program)
  • Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Engineering by Research

Why Choose Mapúa Computer Engineering?

Within five years after their course, graduates of Mapúa's Computer Engineering course in the Philippines should be able to:

  • Address and solve complex engineering challenges through collaborative and individual projects that showcase their ability to innovate and navigate complex problems.
  • Contribute positively to the public welfare by engaging in projects that emphasize health, safety, and environmental sustainability, in compliance with ethical standards and legal requirements.
  • Attain professional growth, evidenced by career advancements or enhanced responsibilities, reflecting the high-level competencies of an engineering undergraduate degree.
  • Commit to lifelong learning through further education, professional development courses, or industrial training, staying current with industry advancements.
  • Excel in technical expertise, professionalism, and ethical practices within ICT and computer systems development, driving significant progress in both industry and society.
